Mission Statement

At Madam Grace W O orphanage we honor the legacy and vision of our founder's mother by providing a safe, nurturing, and supportive environment for vulnerable children. Our mission is to give hope to these young lives empower them with education, healthcare, and unconditional love, helping them grow into confident, capable, and compassionate members of the society who can reach their full potential.

Our Programs

Our Story

Madam Grace Woghiren Obamedo memorial Orphanage home was founded with a simple yet powerful mission: to provide every child with the love, care, and opportunities they deserve. We believe that every child has the potential to achieve greatness when given the right support and environment.

Over the years, we've helped hundreds of children find their path in life through education, emotional support, and a loving community that believes in their potential.
